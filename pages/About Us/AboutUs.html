Our Story

In 2019, we opened our doors here in Downtown Nairobi, Kenya, and since then it has been an exciting ride. We've grown from a tiny startup into one of Nairobi's most popular printing businesses. As we've moved into larger buildings, hired more employees and purchased better printing machines, we have always stayed true to our ideals. Every employee at Bsrat LTD is dedicated to satisfy our customers with the best promotional products, fair prices and friendly service.

You can send us your ready design/art or contact us for help. Whatever method you use we will make sure your project is successful and that you have a great experience dealing with us. We are fully committed to you and your project.
